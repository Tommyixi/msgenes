Noriko Isobe, M.D., Ph.D.

E-mail: Noriko.Isobe@ucsf.edu

Multiple sclerosis (MS) is relatively rare in Japan but there are many patients suffered from various neurological symptoms as same as in other countries. I am quite interested in how much genetic background contributes to MS susceptibility and efficacy of disease modifying therapies. Besides, I and colleagues are working for the association of individual genetic susceptibility or burden and clinical phenotypes.
